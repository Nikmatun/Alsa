Pad Kra Pao

Pad Kra Pow is a very popular Thai food. "Pad" means "fried" and "Kra Pao" is the Thai name of Holy basil that is one of the main ingredients.

Tomyam Seafood

Tomyam is a Thai soup flavoured with fish sauce, lemongrass, lime juice, and chillies. The main ingredients are prawn, mushrooms, and squid.

Harga: Rp40.000

Kimchi Ramyeon

Kimchi ramyeon is a Korean noodle soup. It is made up of tender ramen noodles, mushrooms, garlic, and chunks of kimchi.

Harga: Rp35.000

Tteokbokki

Tteokbokki is a popular korean food. Usually consists of cylinder-shaped rice cakes, sweet red chili sauce, and fish cakes.

Harga: Rp30.000
